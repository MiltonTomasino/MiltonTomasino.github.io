Q: Do you deliver?

A: We offer delivery service through our own website, UberEats, DoorDash, and Fantuan.

Q: Why make a Fisherman's Wok Account?

A: We offer discounts and promotions for our loyal customers who become Fisherman's Wok Members. You can earn points that you can redeem for free items off the menu like our famous Dim Sum and Chow Mein. Also becoming a Fisherman's Wok Member lets you to quickly order food online and pick up an order at the store.

Q: Do you offer catering?

A: We do offer catering services. Please contact us for catering orders at either via email quickyrestaurants@gmail.com or give us a call, our phone number is 510-912-3455.

Q: How do I make reservations?

A: To make reservations for large parties (8 or more people) please contact us ahead of time either via email fishermanswoksf@gmail.com or give us a call, our phone number is 510-912-3455.

Q: Do you offer vegan or vegetarian options? How about substitutions for allergies?

A: We are working towards expanding our menu to include different dietary restrictions, unfortunately we do not have purely chicken, seafood, or vegan options at this time. However, if you do have an allergy please let our wait staff know and we'll accomodate to the best of our ability.
